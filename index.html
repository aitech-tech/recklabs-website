How It Works

Setup in 5 minutes, use forever

The Red Hat model for AI — open-source tools, professionally packaged. You own your data and your setup.

1

Download Installer

One-click Windows installer. Python and Claude Desktop are the only prerequisites.

2

Add Your Credentials

Open config/connectors.json and paste your API keys. They never leave your machine.

3

Restart Claude Desktop

The MCP server starts automatically. You'll see ReckLabs tools appear in Claude Desktop.

4

Authenticate

Ask Claude: "Authenticate with Zoho." A browser window opens for OAuth — one time only.

5

Talk to Your Data

"Show me my recent leads." "Run the pipeline review." Claude does the rest.

Security & Privacy

Your data never leaves your machine

Local-first architecture means full compliance with DPDP Act 2023, CERT-In Directions 2022, and IT Act Section 43A — by design, not by configuration.

🔒

Local-First Architecture

The MCP server runs entirely on your Windows machine. API keys and business data never pass through Recklabs servers. We only validate your license key.

🛡️

AES-256 Skill Encryption

Base skill files are encrypted with AES-256 before distribution. Only your licensed MCP server runtime can decrypt and execute them.

📋

DPDP Act 2023 Compliant

Data minimisation by design. Purpose limitation per connector. No aggregation on our servers. Credential-never-logged policy throughout.

🏗️

CERT-In Ready

Local-first architecture means CERT-In log retention obligations apply only to our operational servers — not to the financial and CRM data flowing through your connectors.

🔄

OAuth2 Token Management

Industry-standard OAuth2 with automatic token refresh. Tokens stored locally in encrypted storage. No passwords stored anywhere.

⚡

Works Offline

Once authenticated, connectors work without internet dependency for supported operations. Your daily workflow is never blocked by our servers going down.
